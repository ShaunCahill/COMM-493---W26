Business Scenario

You are a product manager who just launched a new coffee maker. Customer reviews are flooding in, and you need to quickly understand what people think. This dashboard uses three machine learning models to analyze your feedback: BlazingText classifies each review as positive or negative, then LDA Topic Modeling discovers what customers love (positive topics) and where they see room for improvement (negative topics). Paste your reviews below and click "Analyze Feedback" to get actionable insights.
