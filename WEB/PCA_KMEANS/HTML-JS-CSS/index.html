Business Scenario

You are a real estate market analyst who segments properties into market tiers for pricing guidance and identifying comparable properties. This dashboard uses an AWS SageMaker KMeans clustering model trained on 2,930 Ames, Iowa homes. The model uses PCA to reduce 74 engineered features down to 2 principal components, then groups properties into market tiers. The number of tiers (K) is determined by your model's training — check your notebook's cluster visualization to see how many tiers your model uses. Enter five key property features below and click "Classify Property" to see which market tier the home falls into.
